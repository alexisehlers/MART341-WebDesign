Challah Bread Instructable

  • Why did you choose this project?
    • From the time I was young I would always make Challah bread, I learned from a family friend. Whenever I did it was a kind of therapy for me and the process would give me peace. It was also so yummy and everyone loved it!
  • What does it mean for you?
    • It means that I know I always have something that I can turn to to bring joy to others and ground myself.
