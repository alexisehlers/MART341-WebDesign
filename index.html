1910 Fashion Trends

Hats, hats, more hats

Trends of the 1910's were extremely different from today. In modern times, many people have the Airforce 10 white shoes which are one of the most popular shoes for younger people. Back in 1910, hats, long dresses, feathers and much more were sought after as the latest fashion.

Draping dresses

Fashion in 1910 for women featured long, straight dresses with rich colors. The hair was most often worn up, as women were expected to look tidy and dignified.
