Lexi's Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plus 2 tbs flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking sods
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 stick butter
  • 1 stick margarine
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Mix butter, sugar and salt.
  2. Add egg to mixture and beat in
  3. Mix in all other ingredients gradually
  4. Cook at 350F for 10 mins and enjoy!
